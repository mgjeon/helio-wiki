# ATHARV

ATHARV — Analysis Tool for Heliospheric Arrangement of Remapped Vectors — is introduced as a visualization/analysis tool for interplanetary coronal mass ejections (ICMEs), focused on reconstructing or displaying magnetic structure from in-situ spacecraft sampling [arXiv:2606.01893](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01893).

## Scientific purpose

- Addresses the limitation that spacecraft provide essentially one-dimensional cuts through ICME magnetic fields while geoeffectiveness depends on the three-dimensional magnetic-field structure and orientation [arXiv:2606.01893](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01893).
- Relevant to CME/ICME interpretation, forecast validation, and post-event analysis in [[cme-structure-models]] and [[cme-tools]].

## Inputs and outputs

- Inputs: in-situ ICME magnetic-field vectors sampled along spacecraft trajectories, then remapped for structural visualization [arXiv:2606.01893](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01893).
- Outputs: visualizations of magnetic arrangement/orientation useful for ICME structure interpretation [arXiv:2606.01893](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01893).
