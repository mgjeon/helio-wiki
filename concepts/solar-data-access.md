# Solar Data Access

The supplied sources include GONG/NSO magnetic maps, JSOC, DRMS, HAPI, NOAA SWPC products, LASCO, STEREO, and CCMC model pages as data/model access points. Source: raw/transcripts/Solar-Physics-Codes-Solar-Wind-CME_1.md

- JSOC is represented by the Stanford JSOC site and JSOC-SDP repositories. Source: http://jsoc.stanford.edu/
- NOAA SWPC real-time solar wind and WSA-Enlil products are included. Source: https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/products/real-time-solar-wind and https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/products/wsa-enlil-solar-wind-prediction
- HAPI client access is represented by [[client-python]]. Source: https://github.com/hapi-server/client-python
