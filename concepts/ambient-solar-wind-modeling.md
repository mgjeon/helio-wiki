# Ambient Solar Wind Modeling

Ambient solar-wind model workflows in the supplied source list combine photospheric magnetic maps, coronal-field models, WSA-style coupling, and heliospheric propagation models. Source: raw/transcripts/Solar-Physics-Codes-Solar-Wind-CME_1.md

- Validation is explicitly represented by “Unifying the validation of ambient solar wind models”. Source: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.asr.2022.05.026
- Real-time in-situ solar-wind observations are represented by NOAA SWPC's real-time solar-wind product. Source: https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/products/real-time-solar-wind
- Model/software entries in this wiki include [[pfss]], [[pfsspy]], [[sunkit-magex]], [[mas]], [[swig]], [[hux]], and [[huxt]]. Source: raw/transcripts/Solar-Physics-Codes-Solar-Wind-CME_1.md
