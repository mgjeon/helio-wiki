# Glossary

Concise definitions for recurring terms in this wiki. Definitions should stay short and link to fuller concept, comparison, or software pages.

| Term | Definition | Related pages |
| --- | --- | --- |
| PFSS | Potential Field Source Surface model; a current-free coronal magnetic-field approximation with an outer source surface where field lines become radial. | [[pfss]], [[pfsspy]], [[sunkit-magex]], [[pfss-tools]] |
| NLFFF | Non-Linear Force-Free Field model; a magnetic-field reconstruction where electric currents are allowed and the force-free parameter can vary spatially. | [[nf2]], [[nlfff-tools]], [[coronal-magnetic-field-modeling]] |
| QSL | Quasi-Separatrix Layer; a region where magnetic field-line connectivity changes rapidly and reconnection is often studied. | [[qsl-and-field-line-topology]], [[qsl-tools]] |
| Squashing factor Q | A metric used to quantify how strongly neighboring magnetic field lines diverge in connectivity mapping. | [[fastqsl]], [[fastqsl2]], [[ufit]] |
| DEM | Differential Emission Measure; a temperature distribution of emitting coronal plasma inferred from multi-wavelength or spectroscopic observations. | [[differential-emission-measure]], [[dem-tools]] |
| PINN | Physics-Informed Neural Network; a neural-network model trained with physical-equation constraints as part of the loss function. | [[nf2]], [[pinn-me]] |
| NeRF | Neural Radiance Field; a neural representation of a 3D emitting scene, adapted in this wiki mainly for solar tomographic reconstruction. | [[sunerf]] |
| MAS | Magnetohydrodynamic Algorithm outside a Sphere; a Predictive Science MHD code for modeling the corona and heliosphere. | [[mas]], [[solar-wind-tools]] |
| SHARP | Space-weather HMI Active Region Patch; an HMI active-region data product with magnetic-field quantities and space-weather keywords. | [[sharps]], [[active-region-parameters]] |
| SMARP | Space-weather MDI Active Region Patch; an MDI-era active-region patch product analogous in role to SHARP. | [[smarps]], [[active-region-parameters]] |
| HUX | Heliospheric Upwind eXtrapolation model; a reduced-physics solar-wind propagation model. | [[hux]], [[huxt]], [[solar-wind-tools]] |
| DBM | Drag-Based Model; a simplified CME propagation model using drag interaction with ambient solar wind. | [[dbm]], [[cme-tools]], [[cme-propagation-models]] |
